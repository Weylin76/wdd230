Site Name

Mount Crumpit Chamber of Commerce

The name was selected becuase the name clearly relates back to the purpose of the website

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to educate out-of-state visitors about what Mount Crumpit has to offer. Additionally, the site serves as an excellent resource for locals to be aware of upcoming events.

Scenarios

Visitors will be able to quickly find general information about trail hikes, making their planning process easier and more efficient.

The website will inform visitors about local dining and accommodation options, providing a comprehensive guide to the area's amenities.

Locals can visit the site to stay updated on upcoming board meetings and opportunities to take an active role in community growth and development.
