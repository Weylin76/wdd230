Home Design Wire Frame

Site Name: Mount Crumpit Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

The name was selected becuase the name clearly relates back to the purpose of the website

Site Purpose: The purpose of this site is to educate out-of-state visitors about what Mount Crumpit has to offer. Additionally, the site serves as an excellent resource for locals to be aware of upcoming events.

    Scenarios:
  • Visitors will be able to quickly find general information about trail hikes, making their planning process easier and more efficient.
  • The website will inform visitors about local dining and accommodation options, providing a comprehensive guide to the area's amenities.
  • Locals can visit the site to stay updated on upcoming board meetings and opportunities to take an active role in community growth and development.

Color Schema: The color scheme draws inspiration from the iconic palette associated with the Grinch, featuring vibrant and engaging hues that capture the character's unique charm.

Typography: Our typography embraces a fun, unique, and whimsical style, reflecting the playful essence of our theme while ensuring readability and visual appeal.

Wireframe: The wireframe is designed to be versatile, accommodating multiple viewport types including mobile, tablet, and desktop, ensuring a seamless and responsive user experience across all devices.
